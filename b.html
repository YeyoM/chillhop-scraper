chillhop beat tapes: Teddy Roxpin

Teddy Roxpin

STREAM / BUY SAVE REMOVE

Teddy Roxpin

Producer and multi-instrumentalist Teddy Roxpin continues to impress us. From his 2021 collaborative LP alongside Smile High to his loose singles all year long, he has yet to miss. Now, he levels up with a five song solo beat tape EP. Jazzy and funky and groovy as hell. This is one to play when you pour a cocktail, find a rooftop, and daydream away. 

This project is the third in a series of ongoing beat tapes released through Chillhop Music. Look out for another one soon.
